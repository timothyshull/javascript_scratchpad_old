Introducing Now cards

The right information at just the right time

From knowing the weather before you start your day, to planning the best route to avoid traffic, or even checking your favorite team’s score while they’re playing, get the information you want, when you need it.

Assistance around the clock

Your information is automatically organized into simple cards that appear just when they’re needed. Now cards are ready whenever you are so you can spend less time digging and more time living.

Relevant suggestions

Now cards deliver the information you care about, without you having to search for it. Choose the cards most relevant to you and the experience gets even better, letting you focus on what matters.

You're in control

Now cards work the way you want. Sign in to get customized, highly relevant information. You control the settings that determine the information provided.

Get Google Now on iOS

Download the Google app

Download the Google app from the Apple App Store.

Open the Google app

Once downloaded, the Google app will appear on your phone’s home screen. Tap on the app to open.

Sign in and swipe up

Sign in to your Google account in the top left corner, and then swipe up on the card at the bottom of the screen to start using Google Now.
